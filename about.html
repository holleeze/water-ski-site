ABOUT US

Serving Saskatchewan's water sports community since 1972

OUR STORY

The Saskatoon Water Ski Club (SWSC) is a non-profit organization that has been serving Saskatoon water sports enthusiasts since 1972. What started as a small group of passionate water skiers has grown into one of Saskatchewan's premier water sports facilities.

Located on the beautiful South Saskatchewan River, our club offers world-class conditions for water skiing, wakeboarding, and barefoot skiing. Our dedicated team of coaches and volunteers work tirelessly to share their love of water sports with skiers of all ages and abilities.

Over the decades, we've trained countless athletes who have gone on to compete at provincial, national, and international levels. But whether you're aiming for the podium or just looking to have fun on the water, you'll find a welcoming community at SWSC.
