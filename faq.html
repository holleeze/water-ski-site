FAQ

Answers to commonly asked questions about the club

SEASON INFORMATION

Our season typically runs from late May through early September, weather permitting. The South Saskatchewan River provides excellent conditions for water skiing throughout the summer months.

Operating Hours: Vary by day - contact us for current schedule.

Location: South Saskatchewan River, Saskatoon area.

Questions about membership? Get in touch and we'll be happy to help!

What hours are you open?

End of Season schedule (Aug 18-29) is by booking only.
The Club typically opens the first week of June and closes at the end of August. Check out the Club's Facebook and Instagram pages for updates.
Learn-To Clinics and Tournaments are listed on this website and on the Saskatoon Waterski Club Facebook and Instagram pages.

How much is a Day Pass?

A Day Pass is 60$ CAD, covers insurance and includes 2 tows.
Payment and Waivers are found on the Memberships page of this website.
