MAKING WAVES SINCE 1972

The Saskatoon Water Ski Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving water sports enthusiasts of all skill levels. From your very first time on skis to competitive tournament training, we're here to help you succeed.

Located at "The Rathole", our facility offers world-class conditions and professional instruction in slalom, jump, trick skiing, and wakeboarding
