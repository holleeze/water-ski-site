OUR PROGRAMS

From first-timers to national competitors, we have a program for everyone

BEGINNER LESSONS

Never been on water skis before? No problem! Our beginner program is designed to get you up and skiing in a safe, supportive environment. Our patient instructors specialize in working with first-timers of all ages.

What's included:

Professional instruction, equipment (skis, vest) available if needed, boat time, and a whole lot of encouragement. Most beginners are up on their skis within their first lesson!

Duration: 15-minute sessions

Ages: 6 and up

Price: $15

Book a Beginner Lesson

INTERMEDIATE & ADVANCED TRAINING

For those who understand the foundations of skiing but have an interest in improving through one-on-one dedicated coaching with NCCP-certified coaches and drivers.

Skills we focus on:

First tournament preparation or anyone looking to improve their form and technique.

Duration: 15-minute sessions

Price: $30

Inquire About Training

COMPETITIVE TRAINING

Train with coaches who have competed at provincial, national, and international levels. Our competitive program is for serious athletes looking to compete in tournaments.

Disciplines:

Slalom, trick skiing, and jump. We train athletes for Regional, National, and International competitions. Our facility includes a tournament regulation slalom course and a surveyed jump ramp.

Team Athletes: Our High Performance team trains regularly throughout the season and represents SWSC at tournaments across the World.

Join Competitive Program

WAKEBOARDING

We operate a 2022 Nautique 200 ski boat, perfectly capable of pulling beginner and intermediate wakeboarders who are looking to improve on the basics and learn their first wake tricks. We offer ballast up to 200 additional lbs.

What you'll learn:

Board starts, edge control, wake jumping, surface tricks, and beginner aerial maneuvers. Some equipment available upon request.
