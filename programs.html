OUR PROGRAMS

From first-timers to national competitors, we have a program for everyone

üéø BEGINNER LESSONS

Never been on water skis before? No problem! Our beginner program is designed to get you up and skiing in a safe, supportive environment. Our patient instructors specialize in working with first-timers of all ages.

What's included:

Professional instruction, all equipment (skis, vest, wetsuit if needed), boat time, and a whole lot of encouragement. Most beginners are up on their skis within their first lesson!

Duration: 30-minute sessions

Ages: 6 and up

üìà INTERMEDIATE & ADVANCED TRAINING

Ready to take your skills to the next level? Our intermediate and advanced programs focus on technique refinement, building confidence, and preparing you for more challenging runs.

Skills we focus on:

Deep water starts, crossing the wake, slalom course introduction, speed progression, and building consistency. Our coaches provide video analysis and personalized feedback to accelerate your progress.

Duration: 30-60 minute sessions

Inquire About Training

üèÜ COMPETITIVE TRAINING

Train with coaches who have competed at provincial, national, and international levels. Our competitive program is for serious athletes looking to compete in tournaments.

Disciplines:

Slalom, trick skiing, and jump. We train athletes for regional competitions, the Canada Cup, and beyond. Our facility includes a regulation slalom course and jump ramp.

Team Athletes: Our competitive team trains regularly throughout the season and represents SWSC at tournaments across Canada.

Join Competitive Program

üèÑ WAKEBOARDING

Catch air and learn tricks on our dedicated wakeboard boat! Wakeboarding is a great option for those looking for a different on-water experience. Our instructors can take you from your first board start to landing your first aerial tricks.

What you'll learn:

Board starts, edge control, wake jumping, surface tricks, and aerial maneuvers. Equipment provided for all skill levels.

Book Wakeboard Session

ü¶∂ BAREFOOT SKIING

The ultimate water skiing challenge! Barefoot skiing is exactly what it sounds like - skiing on your bare feet at speeds of 35-45 mph. It's an incredible rush and a unique skill that few master.

Our barefoot program is for experienced water skiers looking for a new challenge. We use specialized equipment and techniques to help you safely learn this exciting discipline.

Learn About Barefooting

üë∂ YOUTH PROGRAMS

We love introducing kids to the joy of water sports! Our youth programs are designed to be fun, safe, and confidence-building. Many of our competitive athletes started in our youth program.

Age groups:

Mini Skiers (6-9), Junior Skiers (10-13), and Teen Skiers (14-17). Each age group has programming appropriate for their development level.

Summer camps: We offer week-long summer camps that combine instruction, games, and plenty of time on the water.
