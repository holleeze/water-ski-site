SEASON INFORMATION

Our season typically runs from late May through early September, weather permitting. Pike Lake provides excellent conditions for water skiing throughout the summer months.

Operating Hours: Vary by day - contact us for current schedule.

Sunset Hours: 4:00 PM - Close (for Sunset memberships).

Location: Pike Lake, Saskatchewan.
